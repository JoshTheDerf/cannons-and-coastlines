Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

What we collect

When you subscribe to our mailing list, we collect the email address you provide. That's it. We don't use analytics or advertising cookies on this website.

How we use it

Your email address is used solely to:

  • Send you the printable rulebook booklet
  • Notify you when new factions are released
  • Share playtest notes and development updates

Our legal basis for processing your email address is your consent, given when you subscribe.

Third-party services

Email delivery is handled by Kit (formerly ConvertKit), acting as our data processor. When you subscribe, your email address is stored on Kit's servers in the United States. Kit may use cookies or tracking pixels to measure email opens and link clicks. Kit's privacy policy is available at kit.com/privacy.

Data retention

Your email address remains on our mailing list until you unsubscribe. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with anyone other than Kit as a data processor.

Your rights

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You may unsubscribe at any time, or email josh@thederf.com to request removal.

You also have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Receive your data in a portable format

EU and UK residents have the right to lodge a complaint with their local data protection authority. California residents have equivalent rights under the CCPA.

Children's privacy

This mailing list is not intended for children under 16. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 16, please email josh@thederf.com and we'll delete it.

International users

Your data will be processed in the United States. This policy is governed by the laws of the State of Georgia, United States.

Changes to this policy

We'll update this policy if our practices change and update the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be communicated via the mailing list.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email josh@thederf.com.
