How to Play

Two players share one device. Player 1 sits at the bottom, Player 2 at the top. In Solo Play, you're P1 and the AI controls P2.

Factions

Each player picks a faction with unique ships, stats, and a passive ability. Factions range from the balanced Queen's Fleet to the lightning-fast Islanders.

On Your Turn

Tap a ship. A menu appears:

⚓ Move — Rings show how far you can sail. Faster factions get more/larger rings.

💥 Fire — Choose Port/Starboard, Bearing, and Elevation to aim your broadside cannons.

🚩/🪙 Island — When touching an island: raise your flag to capture it, or collect a coin from one you own.

Islands

Islands are the economic engine. Capture an uncaptured island by touching it and raising your flag. Collect a coin each turn from islands you own. Islands score victory points!

Coins

You hold 3 coins drawn from a shared bag. Tap a coin, then tap a target ship to spend it. Spent coins return to the bag.

🛡️ Brace — Next hit ignored. 🚩 Signal Flags — Bonus action (free). ⛵ Full Sail — Extended move. ↔️ Evasive — Sideslip. 🎯 Skilled Gunner — Fire twice. 🔧 Repair — +1 mast. ⚔️ Boarding — 1 damage to adjacent enemy.

Combat

HP = masts + 1 (hull). Hits remove masts. 0 masts = "dead in the water" (can fire, can't move). One more hit = scuttled permanently.

Victory

Sink all enemies for instant victory. Or after turn 10, declare victory if you lead in VP: Ships ×3, Islands ×2, Coins ×1, plus ×2 bonuses for leading each category.
